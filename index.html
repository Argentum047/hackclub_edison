What to expect?

You are going meet great people, learn how to make something custom with Computer Science for yourself, and gain the opportunity to contribute to our projects. Not to mention snacks, potential volunteer hours, the ability to create resume and story worth projects and experiences, and most importantly have fun. At the end of the day, our club is whatever you make it because you are the one making.
